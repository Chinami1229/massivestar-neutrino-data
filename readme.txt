
1. folder names: progenitor models
    HOSHI models
    MESA models


2. HOSHI/lightcurvem*.dat
   MESA/lightcurvem*.dat

   * = progenitor ZAMS mass

   These files include the time evolution of number luminosity, luminosity, and average energy.

   array infomation:
    1. step number
    2. time to core bounce [s]
    3. number luminosity of nue [s^{-1}]
    4. number luminosity of nueb [s^{-1}]
    5. number luminosity of nux [s^{-1}]
    6. luminosity of nue [erg/s]
    7. luminosity of nueb [erg/s]
    8. luminosity of nux [erg/s]
    9. average energy of nue [MeV]
    10. average energy of nueb [MeV]
    11. average energy of nux [MeV]


3. HOSHI/@/spectrum*.dat
   MESA/@/spectrum*.dat

   @ = progenitor ZAMS mass
   * = step number in lightcurve files

   array information:
   1. neutrino energy [MeV]
   2. spectrum of nue [MeV^-1 s^-1]
   3. spectrum of nueb [MeV^-1 s^-1]
   4. spectrum of nux [MeV^-1 s^-1]
